Termination Compliance Navigator

Built for Definitely-Not-Evil-Corp Inc. — a decision-support tool for Ontario employment terminations

Not legal advice. This tool provides general guidance to flag compliance risk under Ontario employment law (Employment Standards Act, 2000 and common law). It cannot replace consultation with an employment lawyer, and outputs should be verified against current legislation and case law before any termination decision is finalized.
